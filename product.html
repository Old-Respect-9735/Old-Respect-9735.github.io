Products

These are our products to promote self-mindfulness and wellbeing.


OpenCV Emotion Detector

The Emotion Detector is a code that utilizes an open cv through the user's camera. By finding out the perfect values for a frowning face, we were able to differentiate between a frowning and a happy face. This can be useful for determining a user's mood and brightening their day by making them smile.


OpenCV Hands-off

The Hands-off OpenCV program uses opencv as well as a hand tracking model to detect whether a user touched their face or not. Touching your face is generally unsanitary and can spread viruses, as well as it being an indicator of stress. This can be used to warn the user via auditory warning sounds to remind them not to touch their face.

Here is the autocorrelation model. In Machine Learning, an ARIMA model other known as an Autoregressive Integrated Moving Average model is used to predict the future of a dataset based on previous data. The purpose of an Auto Correlation model is to tell the users how related the data is with respect to one another. This can be fulfilled by using the AD Fuller Test. An AD-Fuller test utilizes the average of the data along with lag models to find a relationship between the data points.
